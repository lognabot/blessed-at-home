Who We Work With

We build lasting relationships with the organizations and professionals who care most about their clients' quality of life. If you're placing someone in need of dependable in-home care, we're ready to be your trusted partner.

🏥

Hospital Discharge Planners & Case Managers

We coordinate closely with discharge teams to ensure smooth, safe transitions from hospital to home — reducing readmissions and giving patients the support they need.

👨‍⚕️

Physicians & Specialists

Primary care providers, geriatricians, neurologists, cardiologists, and oncologists rely on us to extend quality care into the home between appointments.

🏨

Skilled Nursing Facilities & Rehab Centers

We work alongside SNFs and rehabilitation centers to provide continued care as patients transition back into their homes and communities.

🕊

Hospice & Palliative Care Teams

Our caregivers are specially trained in end-of-life support and palliative care, making us a natural partner for hospice organizations and comfort care teams.

🤝

Social Workers & Care Coordinators

Whether hospital-based or community-focused, social workers trust us to follow through on care arrangements with professionalism and compassion.

🎖

Veterans Service Organizations

We proudly accept VA Aid & Attendance benefits and welcome partnerships with VSOs and VA social workers who serve our nation's veterans and surviving spouses.

🏘

Assisted Living Communities

We supplement care for residents in independent and assisted living communities who need additional one-on-one support beyond what the facility provides.

⛪

Faith Communities & Senior Centers

Rooted in faith and community, we partner with local churches, senior centers, and Area Agencies on Aging to reach those who need care most.

What We Offer

A Full Spectrum of In-Home Care

Blessed At Home provides non-medical in-home care for seniors and adults with disabilities. Every care plan is uniquely crafted for the individual — no two plans are alike.

🧍

Personal Care

Bathing, grooming, dressing, oral care, and hygiene assistance delivered with dignity and respect.

♿

Mobility Assistance

Walking assistance, wheelchair support, transferring, and paraplegia/quadriplegia care for safety and independence.

🍽

Feeding & Nutrition

Meal planning, preparation for special diets, and hands-on feeding assistance as needed.

🧹

Homemaking

Light housekeeping, laundry, grocery shopping, and meal preparation to keep the home comfortable and safe.

💬

Companion Care

Meaningful social interaction, engagement with hobbies, cognitive stimulation, and accompaniment on outings.

🚗

Senior Transportation

Escorted transportation to medical appointments, grocery runs, leisure activities, and errands.

💊

Health Reminders

Medication reminders, vital sign monitoring (BP, blood sugar, heart rate), and appointment management.

🧠

Alzheimer's & Dementia Care

Specialized, patient-centered support for those with memory and cognitive changes, focused on normalcy and dignity.

🌿

Palliative & End-of-Life Care

Comfort-focused care for those with serious chronic or life-threatening illness, with support for both client and family.

🌙

24-Hour Care

Around-the-clock in-home care for clients requiring continuous supervision and support.

🌱

Adults With Disabilities

Care for adults with intellectual, developmental, and neuromuscular conditions — enabling safe, independent living.

💪

Wellness Support

Exercise encouragement, cognitive stimulation, meal planning, and safety supervision to promote overall wellbeing.

Funding Options

We Work With Multiple Payment Sources

Navigating payment for home care can be stressful for families. We're well-versed in all available options and can help families understand what they qualify for.

💳

Private Pay

Clients and families paying from personal assets, savings, or investments — the most flexible and immediate option.

📄

Long-Term Care Insurance

LTC insurance policies fund daily, weekly, or annual care costs. We help families verify and activate their coverage.

🎖

Veterans Aid & Attendance

Eligible veterans and surviving spouses can access A&A benefits through the VA in addition to their monthly pension.

🏦

Senior Living Loans

Unsecured loans guaranteed by family members, designed specifically to fund up to three years of long-term care.

🏠

Reverse Mortgage

Homeowners age 62+ can convert home equity into tax-free income to fund care without selling their home.
